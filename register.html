Volunteer coaching

Help Build Vail Velocity

Parents selected to serve as a Head Coach will receive one complimentary player registration (a $135 value) for a child participating during the same season they coach received as a refund after coach placement.

Previous volleyball coaching experience is not required. All selected coaches must complete the Vail Velocity Coach Academy, a virtual online training, before the season begins. Approved coaches may also be required to complete volunteer paperwork or background screening.

Policies and agreements

Practice placement notice

We will use the closest-school information to help place practices near participating families whenever facility availability allows. Practice location, teammate and coach requests are not guaranteed.

Refund policy

A full refund is available before the official season begins. After the official season begins, refunds are limited to 50% of the $135 registration fee. Any payment-processing fees charged by an outside provider may be nonrefundable.
