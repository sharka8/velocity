Frequently asked questions

What families should know.

Does my child need volleyball experience?

No. The opening season welcomes beginners while still giving experienced players room to improve.

Who are the coaches?

Coaches are parent volunteers who receive a virtual training and background check. Our coaches are provided with practice drills and suggestions along with a guide on teaching sportsmanship and mentoring youth athletes.

When and where will practices and games be held?

The league will use available Vail and Sunnyside school-district facilities,striving to use the school closest to each player's home to help create geographically convenient practice groups whenever possible. Practices will be twice per week when possible. For example, if your practices are at Rincon Vista Middle School on Mondays and Thursdays but Monday is a holiday one week then there will only be practice on Thursday that week. Games are generally on Friday evenings but may occassionally be on Thursdays or Saturdays depending on the availabilty of facilities.

Can you guarantee a nearby practice location?

No. Location requests will be considered, but assignments also depend on gym availability, coaches, team balance, age and enrollment.

What is the refund policy?

A full refund is available before the official season begins. After the season begins, refunds are limited to 50% of the registration fee. Any nonrefundable payment-processing fees may be excluded.
