Frequently asked questions

What families should know.

Does my child need volleyball experience?

No. The opening season is intended to welcome beginners while still giving experienced players room to improve.

Are the age divisions final?

The working divisions are ages 9–11 and 12–15. They may be adjusted to create safe, balanced teams.

Where will games be played?

The league is pursuing gym space in Rita Ranch and the broader Vail area.

When will registration open?

Registration will open after the gym schedule and final league price are confirmed.
